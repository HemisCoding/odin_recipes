Grandma's Ground Beef Casserole

Nobody makes casserole like your Grandma. That's why this old-fashioned ground beef casserole with egg noodles is always such a hit! It's made with ingredients you already have on hand (and, of course, a little bit of love).

  1. Ground Beef

    2. Of course, you'll need ground beef for this casserole. The recipe calls for about one pound.

  2. Tomato Sauce

    3. Canned tomato sauce adds flavor and helps bring the casserole together.

  3. Seasonings

    4. White sugar, salt, and garlic salt are used to add flavor to the tomato sauce.
